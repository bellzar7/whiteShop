The way to a healthy body

We know that nutrition is the key to health and well-being, and we are experienced in this field. We believe that everyone has the right to a healthy body and the energy to achieve their dreams. However, too many misconceptions and common myths about nutrition can get in the way. We therefore designed this course to provide our participants with science-based and practical information about good nutrition to help them make informed decisions about their diet and improve their health and well-being.

About the course

Course structure

Basics of healthy nutrition

This section of the course will outline the basic principles of healthy eating, including a variety of foods, rational portions, eating patterns, and the importance of a balanced diet.This will include aspects such as meal planning, choosing healthy foods when shopping.

The role of macro- and microelements in the diet

This chapter will discuss in detail the role of macro- and micronutrients in a healthy diet, including proteins, carbohydrates, fats, vitamins and minerals, and their effect on the functioning of the body.

Optimal consumption of various products

In this section, different groups of foods, such as vegetables, fruits, meat, fish, dairy products, and others, and recommendations for their consumption, according to the needs of the body, will be considered.

Diet planning and diet

This section will outline strategies for planning a healthy diet and eating regimen, including counting calories, dividing your meals throughout the day, and matching your diet to your body's needs.

Recognizing and avoiding food errors

This section will address common eating mistakes such as overeating, junk food, emotional eating, and more, and teach course participants how to recognize and avoid them.

Practical tips for successful nutrition

In this section, practical advice and recommendations will be provided for the successful implementation of healthy nutrition in everyday life.
